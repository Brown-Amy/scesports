Coaches

Do you dream of coaching a championship esport team? Come learn the tools and tactics of great coaches. Help your players develop their skills in game play and during practice. We can show you how to win more consistently.

Parent Volunteers

Become a part of your child's growing experience. No experience required, we will help provide the game knowledge and practice tactics. This is the best way to learn with your child and influence the comradery and sportsmanship experience. Take advantage of all the great perks that come with volunteering. As a volunteer you receive discounted leagues, exclusive access to game film, play time credits, and a free VIP membership. Please email us any questions or speak with a staff member in house.

Staff Mentors

Ready to coach the next championship team? Are you a top level player ready to share your knowledge and grow your coaching skills? Apply today as a staff coach. As a staff coach you will be working with a group of volunteer coaches teaching skills to players and coaches alike. You can also coach a team of your own in our upper divisional leagues and tournaments. Take advantage of our facilities and contacts to grow your coaching career.

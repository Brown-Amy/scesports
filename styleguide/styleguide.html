Salt City e-sports Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to give an online presence to a brick and mortor facility and to drive foot traffic into the store. It is where fans and customers of the facility share information. Place to schedule events and activities. The purpose of this site is to inform esports players of local events, allow them a place to play and meet others, provide coaching opportunites

Target Audience

15-30 year old video game players of any level of experience and their parents as well as those interested in esports. Predominatley single male audience (about 70%) 60% college educuated.

Color Palette

Salt is another name for a pirate which is where the logo originated from. The colors are chosen to represent the pirate flag and the gold hightlight is a reflection of pirates gold. It is also a neutral color for the gaming world. As we want to avoid choosing a specific esports team color.

Typography

To portray the gaming theme I decided to use a monospace font for the headings to represent the original game consoles. This new take on an old concept is fresh and new for hip gamers that like the vintage feel. I paired this with Source Code pro a nice coding font that fits the theme.
